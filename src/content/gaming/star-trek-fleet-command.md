## Overview

Star Trek Fleet Command is a free-to-play mobile and PC MMO strategy game where players build an advanced starbase, command iconic vessels, recruit legendary officers, and navigate conflicts across the Alpha and Beta quadrants.

As with most mobile games, it features in-app purchases and time-limited events. It is also very addictive, and it's not a game you can just leave for a while without consequences, such as losing event rewards, falling behind in progression, or having all your resources raided.

## Alliance

The main social aspect of the game is the alliance system, where players can join together to share resources, coordinate on events and support each other's progression. I have been part of the **Species 8472** alliance for many years, and it's been a great way to connect with other players, share strategies and enjoy the game together.

## Server

Star Trek Fleet Command is available on multiple servers, in multiple regions. It is a pain to select the right server when you start playing, and once you reach a certain point, you can't transfer servers, so you have to be careful with your choice. I play on the **European server 132**.
