## Frak Stars Overview

Frak Stars is [Holosuite Media](/projects/podcasting/holosuite-media)'s dedicated Battlestar Galactica podcast, exploring one of science fiction's most acclaimed and thought-provoking franchises.

From episode reviews and character analysis to discussions on politics, religion, military strategy and artificial intelligence, Frak Stars examines the many themes that made Battlestar Galactica far more than a traditional space opera.

The show covers all corners of the franchise, including:

- Battlestar Galactica (2004)
- The Miniseries
- Razor
- The Plan
- Blood & Chrome
- Caprica
- The wider lore, ships, technology and Cylon mythology

Whether you're a long-time fan of the Fleet or discovering the series for the first time, Frak Stars aims to provide insightful discussion, passionate debate and a few laughs along the journey.

### Returning Soon

Following an extended hiatus that began in 2016 due to personal commitments, Frak Stars is currently preparing for its long-awaited return in 2027.
