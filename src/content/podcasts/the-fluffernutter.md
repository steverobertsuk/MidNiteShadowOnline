## The Fluffernutter Overview

The Fluffernutter is Holosuite Media’s podcast dedicated to the Expeditionary Force universe created by Craig Alanson.

Hosted by me (Steve) and my friend Nathan, the show follows humanity’s adventures alongside the irreverent, all-powerful AI known as Skippy the Magnificent. Each episode explores the books, characters, species, battles, technology and unforgettable moments that have made Expeditionary Force one of the most popular modern military science fiction series.

Expect in-depth discussion, analysis, theories, humour and the occasional tangent that somehow starts with space warfare and ends somewhere completely unexpected.

Topics covered include:

- The Expeditionary Force novels
- Character and species discussions
- Military strategy and fleet actions
- Alien politics and interstellar conflicts
- Skippy's many questionable decisions
- Fan theories and future story speculation

### Your Monkeys

#### Nathan

Nathan has been a devoted Expeditionary Force fan since the series began. Having listened to the audiobooks more times than he can count, he brings encyclopaedic knowledge of the books and spends much of his time attempting to keep discussions on track.

Results vary.

#### Steve

A lifelong science fiction fan, podcaster and self-confessed technology enthusiast, Steve brings enthusiasm, curiosity and occasional chaos to every episode. If Skippy ever met him in person, there is a reasonable chance he would be ejected through the nearest airlock for operational efficiency.
