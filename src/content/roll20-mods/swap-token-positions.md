## Overview

SwapTokenPositions is aimed at tables that frequently use forced movement, teleports, repositioning powers, or GM adjudication that requires instant token swaps.

Instead of dragging tokens manually and risking overlap, the mod will validate two target tokens, exchange their coordinates, and preserve layer placement with minimal friction.

## Release notes

### Planned release

- Swap the positions of exactly two selected tokens.
- Preserve token layer, size, and rotation values during the move.
- Provide clear chat feedback when the selection is invalid or incomplete.
