## Overview

Condition Tracker is designed to reduce the bookkeeping load around common combat effects such as stunned, poisoned, restrained, or custom table-specific statuses.

The mod focuses on keeping state visible, associating durations with tokens, and providing predictable expiry handling as initiative advances.

## Release notes

### Current development target

- Token-linked condition entries with optional round counters.
- GM-facing commands to add, remove, and inspect active effects.
- Turn progression hooks to decrement timed conditions automatically.
