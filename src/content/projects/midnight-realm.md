Midnight Realm is a homebrew tabletop concept designed as a modular series of one-shots with a strong atmosphere and repeatable structure.

The setting focuses on exploration, survival, strange light mechanics and recurring artefacts such as the Orb of Osuvox. It can become a showcase for campaign design, mechanics, maps, tokens, lore and session preparation.

## Content ideas

- Region overview
- Episode structure
- Orb mechanics
- Inspiration rules
- Pre-generated character notes
- Maps, tokens and VTT setup
