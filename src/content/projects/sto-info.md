STO Info is a substantial development project sitting at the intersection of Star Trek Online, community tooling and modern web engineering.

The project is built around a separate frontend and backend, with a public-facing app, API, health monitoring, release processes and a growing feature roadmap. It is the most direct example of MidNiteShadow as a technical builder inside a gaming community.
