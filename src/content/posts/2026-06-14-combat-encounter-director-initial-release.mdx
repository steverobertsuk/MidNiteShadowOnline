I'm pleased to announce the release of **Combat Encounter Director**, a new Roll20 Mod designed to give Game Masters greater control over the flow of combat encounters.

No matter how carefully an encounter is prepared, things can change quickly at the table. Groups may be smaller than expected, reinforcements might be needed, bosses can prove less threatening than intended, or an accidental token movement can disrupt a carefully planned encounter.

Combat Encounter Director was created to help address those situations without interrupting the pace of the game.

## Built for flexibility

The mod provides a collection of encounter management tools designed to help Game Masters adapt quickly and confidently during play.

Key features include:

- Dynamic party scaling for groups ranging from solo adventures to convention-sized tables.
- Boss and minion presets to adjust the role and threat level of creatures instantly.
- Reinforcement tools for duplicating and numbering enemy tokens.
- Layer and visibility controls for revealing ambushes and managing the battlefield.
- Position saving and restoration for encounter testing and recovery.
- Encounter templates for storing and reusing complete encounter setups.
- Reset and recovery options that preserve original token values.
- Journal-based reporting to track encounter changes throughout a session.

All of these tools are accessible through an automatically generated **Command Deck**, providing a fast and intuitive interface directly within Roll20.

## Designed with confidence in mind

One of the core goals behind Combat Encounter Director was to make encounter adjustments feel safe.

Many of the mod's features preserve original values behind the scenes, allowing Game Masters to experiment with scaling, apply presets or make tactical changes knowing that they can restore tokens or even entire encounters if needed.

The result is a toolkit that encourages flexibility without sacrificing control.

## Available now

Combat Encounter Director is available today in the Roll20 One-Click installer and is fully compatible with Roll20 API campaigns, including Jumpgate environments.

You can learn more about the mod, explore the full feature set and access download links on the project page below:

➡️ [**Combat Encounter Director Project Page**](https://midniteshadow.online/projects/roll20-mods/combat-encounter-director/)

Feedback and suggestions are always welcome, and future updates will continue to refine and expand the tools available to Game Masters.

I hope Combat Encounter Director helps make encounter preparation and management a little easier, allowing you to spend less time wrestling with administration and more time focusing on the story unfolding at your table.
